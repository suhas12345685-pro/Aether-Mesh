⚡ How it works

Four steps. Zero prompts.

Aether Mesh runs its heartbeat loop every 60 seconds — watching, thinking, and delivering while your team focuses on what matters.

🔌
1
Connects

Joins Slack, Teams, or WhatsApp as a native team member. No bot token limitations — it's a real account.

👁️
2
Watches

Monitors every conversation, detects open tasks, questions, and requests using semantic understanding.

🧠
3
Thinks

Your BYOB LLM brain — GPT-4, Claude, or local Ollama — crafts a complete, context-aware solution.

📦
4
Delivers

Posts the finished work — code, docs, summaries, replies — directly back into the channel where it was needed.

🔒 Architecture

Bring Your Own Brain

You choose the LLM. You hold the keys. Aether Mesh is just the body — connecting your brain to your channels and doing the work.

🔒 Your API keys never leave your machine
🤖
The Body

The core agent loop: heartbeat scheduler, channel reader, task detector, deliverable sender. Runs locally or in our zero-knowledge cloud.

🧠
The Brain

Plug in GPT-4o, Claude 3.5 Sonnet, Gemini Pro, or any local Ollama model. Swap providers without downtime. Your API key, your cost.

📈
The Self-Learning Harness

Task outcomes are scored and stored in a local vector DB. Aether learns your team's preferences and improves every heartbeat cycle.
